To activate the full wallet feature:  
1\. Setup or access a wallet on blockchain.info/wallet from your computer.  
2\. Connect your iPhone to the internet via the same IP address that you
accessed the wallet on your computer from.  
3\. Install the block chain app.  
4\. The app will have full wallet functionality. Create a new account, or pair
it with an existing one.  
  
  
If you don't follow the steps above, it will only allow you to view bitcoin
transactions.
