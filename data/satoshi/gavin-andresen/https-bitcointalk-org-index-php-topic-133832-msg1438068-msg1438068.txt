 **Is "Shared Wallet" the right metaphor?  
**  
I use the term "shared wallet" to mean: one or more multisignature bitcoin
addresses that can receive funds and whose private keys are distributed to
more than one person or device.  
  
Perhaps "joint account" or some other banking term would be better.
