Last July, BIP 34 was accepted. It specifies a "soft fork":  
  
  
We are getting close to that threshold: 821 out of the latest 1000 blocks were
version 2.  
  
If you are mining in a pool: there is a list of pools and what versions they
are producing here. If your pool is producting version=1 blocks, you should
urge your pool operator to upgrade or patch.  
  
If you are mining with p2pool or solo and using a very old version of
bitcoind: you should upgrade, or you risk your blocks getting orphaned.
